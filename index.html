Productivity Tips

The Pomodoro Technique

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method that uses a timer to break work into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks. Each interval is known as a pomodoro.

GengarHour User Manual

Getting Started

Welcome to GengarHour! This Pomodoro timer helps you boost productivity using the proven Pomodoro Technique.

Pomodoro Mode

25-minute focused work sessions (customizable)

Short Break

5-minute breaks between work sessions

Long Break

15-minute breaks after every 4 Pomodoros

Basic Operation

  • Select your desired mode (Pomodoro, Short Break, or Long Break)
  • Click Start to begin the timer
  • Use Pause to temporarily stop the timer
  • Click Reset to restart the current session
  • Sessions completed counter increases after each Pomodoro

Customization

Personalize your timer experience:

  • Adjust session durations in the Settings tab
  • Toggle between Dark/Light mode using the moon icon
  • Customize colors with the palette icon
  • All settings are automatically saved

Tracking & Achievements

Monitor your productivity progress:

  • View daily, weekly, and total session statistics
  • Track your focus time in hours and minutes
  • Unlock achievements for reaching milestones
  • Maintain streaks for consistent usage

Productivity Tips

Visit the Tips tab for valuable advice:

  • Learn about the Pomodoro Technique
  • Discover ways to minimize distractions
  • Get task planning strategies
  • Learn effective break techniques

Pro Tip

The timer automatically cycles between Pomodoro sessions and breaks. After every 4 Pomodoros, you'll get a longer break to recharge!
